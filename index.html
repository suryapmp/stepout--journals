What we Do?

Social and emotional learning (SEL) is “the process through which children and adults, acquire and effectively apply the knowledge, attitudes, and skills necessary to understand and manage emotions, set and achieve positive goals, feel and show empathy for others, establish and maintain positive relationships and make responsible decisions.”

5 core competencies of SEL: self-awareness, self-management, social awareness, relationship skills and responsible decision making

Welcome to Stepout Journals

Stepout Journals provide the space and support for children and adults to develop their socio-emotional skills, who go on to make a positive and lasting impact within them and the society on a wider scale.

At Stepout Journals, children and adults are nurtured with social and emotional competencies through travel/activities/experiences using reflective learning practices and active dialoguing. This enables them to have a positive outlook towards their own identities, recognize the impact of their actions and make active change in their communities.
