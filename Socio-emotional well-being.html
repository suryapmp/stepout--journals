Socio-emotional well-being (Age - 6 to 9 years)

A series of workshops with games, activities, storytelling and experiences focussing on five core competencies of social emotional learning.

We believe that a child’s growth isn’t only determined by cognitive skills, as once thought. Social and emotional abilities, being indicators of how well a person empathizes with his or her environment, adapts to change, exhibits resilience and functions in the society are very much critical in today’s fast paced world with new and prevalent emotional realities. These skills are identified as “The missing piece in a child’s development” and “Future of Education”
