Stepout Learning Journeys – Wilderness

Stepout Learning Journeys – Wilderness

Outdoor adventure experiences to encourage participants to step out of comfort zone, be independent, stretch themselves physically and mentally by introducing wilderness in a safe yet unfamiliar environment

We provide less structured yet safe spaces that offers plenty of socio-emotional exploration opportunities for growing children.

Experiences

  • Camping.

  • Team building activities

  • Exploring local flora fauna.

  • Trekking

  • Cycling in the trails.

  • Local history and culture.

  • Exploring local food

  • Fun games

  • Visit to neighbouring villages.

  • Creatives tasks to know fellow participants

  • Reflection and introspection activities

Skills

  • Learning to listen and act

  • Building coordination

  • Self-management

  • Time management

  • Decision making

  • Critical thinking

  • Creative thinking

  • Mindfulnesst

  • Problem solving

  • Social & Environmental awareness

  • Resilience

  • Challenge and risk taking
